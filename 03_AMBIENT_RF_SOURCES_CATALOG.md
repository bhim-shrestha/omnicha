# Ambient RF Sources: Comprehensive Catalog

## 1. Overview of Omnipresent Electromagnetic Energy

### The Invisible Energy Sea
We live in a soup of electromagnetic radiation. Every wireless device, broadcast tower, and electrical system contributes to ambient RF energy.

## 2. Common RF Sources by Location

### INSIDE YOUR HOME

#### WiFi Routers
| Parameter | Value | Harvestable? |
|-----------|-------|--------------|
| **Frequency** | 2.4 GHz, 5 GHz, 6 GHz (WiFi 6E) | Yes |
| **Power Output** | 100-1000 mW | Good |
| **Range** | 50-100m | Limited by walls |
| **Availability** | 24/7 | Excellent |
| **Power Density @ 1m** | 10-100 μW/m² | Moderate |

**Multiple WiFi networks in apartments = Additive power!**

#### Cellular Signals (Indoor)
| Band | Frequency | Power Density | Penetration |
|------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| **4G LTE** | 700-2600 MHz | 1-10 μW/m² | Good |
| **5G Sub-6** | 3.5 GHz | 5-20 μW/m² | Moderate |
| **5G mmWave** | 24-39 GHz | Variable | Poor |

#### Bluetooth Devices
- **Frequency**: 2.4 GHz
- **Power**: 1-100 mW
- **Range**: 10-100m
- **Power Density**: Very low (0.1-1 μW/m²)
- **Sources**: Phones, speakers, smart home devices

#### Microwave Oven (When Operating)
- **Frequency**: 2.45 GHz (same as WiFi!)
- **Power**: 1000W (but shielded)
- **Leakage**: <5 mW/cm² at 5cm (legal limit)
- **Harvestable**: Minimal (good shielding)

#### Smart Home Devices
- **ZigBee**: 2.4 GHz
- **Z-Wave**: 915 MHz (US)
- **LoRa**: 915 MHz (US)
- **Thread**: 2.4 GHz
- **Matter**: 2.4 GHz

#### Baby Monitors
- **Analog**: 49 MHz, 900 MHz
- **Digital**: 2.4 GHz
- **Power**: 10-100 mW

#### Cordless Phones
- **DECT**: 1.9 GHz
- **Power**: 100 mW
- **Still common in homes**

#### TV/Radio Reception
- **FM Radio**: 88-108 MHz (external source)
- **Digital TV**: 470-806 MHz (external source)
- **Satellite**: 12 GHz (very focused)

### Multi-Band Strategy 
**CRITICAL INSIGHT**: Harvest ALL bands simultaneously!

```
Total Power = Σ(Power from each band)

With 10 frequency bands:
If each yields 10 μW/m² → Total = 100 μW/m²

With large antenna array (1m²):
100 μW/m² × 1m² × 40% efficiency = 40 μW harvested
```
