## Consumer Apps & Products

### Reality Check: Almost NONE for Ambient RF!

**Why No Consumer Products Yet:**
1. **Power too low** for phones/wearables
2. **Requires dedicated transmitters** for useful power
3. **Regulatory challenges** (FCC limits)
4. **User expectations** too high
5. **Competition from batteries** (cheap, proven)

### Closest Consumer Examples:

#### **1. NFC-Powered Devices** (Sort of...)
- **Examples:** Contactless payment cards, NFC tags
- **Power:** From reader's RF field
- **Range:** <10cm
- **Note:** Very short range, not ambient

#### **2. Passive RFID Tags** (Everywhere!)
- **Examples:** Store security tags, inventory tags
- **Power:** From RFID reader
- **Range:** 1-10 meters
- **Market:** Massive ($10B+)
- **Note:** Needs powered reader nearby

#### **3. Qi Wireless Charging** (Mainstream)
- **Examples:** iPhone, Samsung, AirPods charging pads
- **Power:** 5-15W
- **Range:** Contact only
- **Market:** Huge ($10B+)
- **Note:** Inductive, not RF!
