3. **Piñuela, M., Mitcheson, P. D., & Lucyszyn, S. (2013).** "Ambient RF Energy Harvesting in Urban and Semi-Urban Environments." *IEEE Transactions on Microwave Theory and Techniques*, 61(7), 2715-2726.

## Methodology Notes

### Research Approach

This research project used:
- **Literature review**: IEEE Xplore, Google Scholar, patent databases
- **Product analysis**: Commercial datasheets and technical specifications
- **Market research**: Industry reports from McKinsey, Gartner, IDTechEx
- **Calculations**: Based on published formulas (Friis equation, Planck-Einstein relation)
- **Simulations**: Custom Python simulator using industry-standard equations

### Verification

All power density values, efficiency claims, and technical specifications were cross-referenced with:
- Multiple academic papers (peer-reviewed)
- Commercial product datasheets
- Regulatory standards (FCC, ETSI)
- Independent measurements published in journals
